# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors are expected to keep collaboration factual, respectful, and focused on improving the software.

Examples of acceptable behavior:

- constructive technical feedback
- respectful disagreement with clear reasoning
- assuming good intent while challenging weak assumptions

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- repeated bad-faith disruption of review or issue threads

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or restrict participation when conduct makes collaboration unsafe or unproductive.
